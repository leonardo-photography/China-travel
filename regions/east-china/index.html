East China

Over a thousand years ago, this area was the main terminus of the Maritime Silk Road, and traded extensively with Japan and Southeast Asia. The region has not only played a significant role historically but it has also become an economic powerhouse beyond even the Pearl River Delta in South China. East China comprises of the following seven provinces and municipality: Anhui, Fujian, Jiangsu, Jiangxi, Shandong, Zhejiang, Taiwan, and municipality of Shanghai. The area has many millions of migrant workers from less developed areas of China.
